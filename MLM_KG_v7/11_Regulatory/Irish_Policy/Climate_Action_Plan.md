# Climate Action Plan 2024 (Ireland)

## Summary
Ireland's annual statutory Climate Action Plan under the Climate Action and Low Carbon Development Act 2021. Sets sectoral carbon budgets and annual emissions ceilings. Buildings and electricity sectors are primary MLM relevance areas.

## Key Targets Relevant to MLM

| Sector | Target | 2030 Goal | MLM Model |
|---|---|---|---|
| Electricity | 80% renewable electricity | 80% RES-E by 2030 | [[02_Models/R_Models/R01]], [[02_Models/R_Models/R02]], [[02_Models/P_Models/P06]] |
| Buildings | 680,000 retrofits to BER B2 or better | 45% reduction in building emissions | [[02_Models/K_Models/K02]], [[02_Models/T_Models/T11]], [[02_Models/T_Models/T19]] |
| Heat pumps | 400,000 heat pump installations | Replace oil/gas heating | [[02_Models/T_Models/T03]], [[02_Models/T_Models/T02]] |
| EVs | 845,000 EVs on road | Displace fossil transport | [[02_Models/B_Models/B03]], [[02_Models/B_Models/B04]], [[02_Models/B_Models/B05]] |
| Smart metering | National smart meter rollout | Enable demand response | [[03_Datasets/DS-SMART-METER]], [[02_Models/L_Models/L01]] |

## MLM Models That Demonstrate Compliance
- **[[02_Models/P_Models/P06]]** — PED Performance KPI: demonstrates community net renewable surplus
- **[[02_Models/K_Models/K01]]** — Avoided Carbon: quantifies annual CO₂ avoided vs CAP baseline
- **[[02_Models/K_Models/K04]]** — Carbon Budget Tracker: tracks against sectoral carbon budget allocation
- **[[02_Models/T_Models/T03]]** — Heat Pump Thermal Model: supports 400,000 HP target
- **[[02_Models/B_Models/B04]], [[02_Models/B_Models/B05]]** — EV charging and V2G: support EV target + grid integration
- **[[02_Models/M_Models/M08]]** — Carbon Intensity Forecast: uses SEAI annual emission factors aligned with CAP

## ODEON/O-CEI Relevance
The O-CEI pilot on Aran Islands is referenced in CAP context as an island energy community model. Aran Islands decarbonisation aligns with the climate planning framework for remote island communities.
