# W18 — Grid-Stress Weather Indicator

## Purpose
Grid-Stress Weather Indicator in the [[W-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$WSI_{grid} = w_1 \cdot f(T_{out}) + w_2 \cdot f(v_{wind}) + w_3 \cdot f(G_{POA})$$

### Description
Weather-to-grid stress index combining temperature extremes (high HVAC demand), low wind (low DER output), and low irradiance into a composite grid risk score.

### Method
Composite index, threshold rules
