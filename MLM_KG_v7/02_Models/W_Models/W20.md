# W20 — OKTAS-to-PV Availability Mapping Model

## Purpose
OKTAS-to-PV Availability Mapping Model in the [[W-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P_{PV,avail} = G_{POA,clear} \cdot (1 - k_{cloud} \cdot OKTAS/8) \cdot \eta_{PV}$$

### Description
Translates OKTAS cloud score directly to available PV power fraction. Fast lookup used in real-time dispatch to estimate PV availability without full W01 computation.

### Method
Empirical lookup, regression
