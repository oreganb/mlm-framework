# W03 — Wind Speed & Direction Model

## Purpose
Wind Speed & Direction Model in the [[W-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$f(v) = \frac{k}{\lambda}\left(\frac{v}{\lambda}\right)^{k-1} e^{-(v/\lambda)^k}$$

### Description
Wind speed modelled as Weibull distribution. Shape k and scale λ fitted from local historical data. Direction modelled as von Mises distribution.

### Method
Weibull distribution, ARIMA, ensemble
