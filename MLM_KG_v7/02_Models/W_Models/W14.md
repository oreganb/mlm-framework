# W14 — Weather Ensemble Generator

## Purpose
Weather Ensemble Generator in the [[W-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$\mathbf{w}^{(\omega)} = \bar{\mathbf{w}} + L \boldsymbol{\epsilon}^{(\omega)}, \quad \boldsymbol{\epsilon} \sim \mathcal{N}(\mathbf{0}, \mathbf{I})$$

### Description
Ensemble of N=50 weather realisations generated by perturbing NWP mean with Cholesky decomposition L of covariance matrix Σ. Preserves cross-variable correlations.

### Method
Monte Carlo, NWP ensemble perturbation
