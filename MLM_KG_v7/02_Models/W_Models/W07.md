# W07 — Atmospheric Pressure Model

## Purpose
Atmospheric Pressure Model in the [[W-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P_{atm}(t) = P_0 \cdot e^{-Mgh/RT}$$

### Description
Station pressure corrected for altitude using barometric formula. Time series evolution via ARIMA(1,1,1) on hourly observations.

### Method
ARIMA, barometric formula
