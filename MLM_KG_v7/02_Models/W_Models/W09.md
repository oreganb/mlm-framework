# W09 — Coastal & Marine Conditions Model

## Purpose
Coastal & Marine Conditions Model in the [[W-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$T_{marine} = T_{out} - k_{sea} \cdot (T_{out} - T_{SST})$$

### Description
Marine temperature correction: sea surface temperature (SST) moderates coastal air temperature. Salt spray and humidity affect panel soiling rate.

### Method
Statistical, Met Éireann marine forecast
