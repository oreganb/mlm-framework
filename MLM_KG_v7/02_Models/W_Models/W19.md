# W19 — Islanding Condition Weather Model

## Purpose
Islanding Condition Weather Model in the [[W-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P_{island\_weather} = P(v_{wind} > v_{thresh}) \cdot P(G_{POA} > G_{thresh})$$

### Description
Joint probability that weather conditions support autonomous islanded operation: sufficient wind OR solar to meet local load without grid.

### Method
Threshold rules, composite assessment

## Weights & Parameters

- **W-W19-VTHRESH:** v_wind threshold for viable wind gen = 4 m/s
- **W-W19-GTHRESH:** G_POA threshold = 200 W/m² (20% of peak)
