# R05 — Inverter Efficiency Model

## Purpose
Inverter Efficiency Model in the [[R-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$\eta_{inv}(P_{DC}) = \eta_{max} \cdot \frac{P_{DC}/P_{rated}}{p_1 + p_2 \cdot P_{DC}/P_{rated} + p_3 \cdot (P_{DC}/P_{rated})^2}$$

### Description
Sandia inverter efficiency model as function of DC power fraction. Efficiency peaks at 80–90% of rated power; drops at low and rated power.

### Method
Empirical efficiency curve, interpolation

## Weights & Parameters

- **W-R05-ETA_MAX:** η_max = 0.97 (modern SMA/Fronius inverters)
- **W-R05-P1:** p1=0.025, p2=0.01, p3=0.005 (Sandia model coefficients)
