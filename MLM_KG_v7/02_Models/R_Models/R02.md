# R02 — Wind Generation Model

## Purpose
Wind Generation Model in the [[R-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P_{wind}(v) = \frac{1}{2} \rho A C_p v^3, \quad \text{clipped to rated power}$$

### Description
Wind turbine power from wind speed using manufacturer power curve. Cubic relationship clipped at rated power and cut-in/cut-out limits.

### Method
Power curve, Weibull distribution
