# O07 — EV Departure/Return Model

## Purpose
EV Departure/Return Model in the [[O-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P(depart | t) = \pi_{dep}(t, schedule), \quad SoC_{return} \sim \mathcal{N}(\mu_{SoC}, \sigma_{SoC}^2)$$

### Description
EV departure and return times modelled as Markov chain conditioned on occupant schedule. SoC on return drawn from Gaussian distribution of trip energy use.

### Method
Markov chain, trip diary statistics
