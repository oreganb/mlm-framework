# O05 — Appliance Use Model

## Purpose
Appliance Use Model in the [[O-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P(appliance_i \text{ on}|t) = \pi_i(t) \cdot occ\_state(t)$$

### Description
Appliance on/off probability as Markov chain modulated by occupancy and time-of-day. Separate chains per appliance type.

### Method
Markov chain, time-use survey weighting
