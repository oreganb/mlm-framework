# O02 — Occupant Schedule Model

## Purpose
Occupant Schedule Model in the [[O-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P(schedule=s) = \begin{cases} 0.60 & s = \text{9-5 worker} \\ 0.30 & s = \text{shift worker} \\ 0.10 & s = \text{retiree} \end{cases}$$

### Description
Occupant schedule archetype sampled from national distribution. Determines baseline occupancy probability profile shape for O01 priors.

### Method
Markov chain, empirical schedule distribution
