# O09 — Natural Ventilation Model

## Purpose
Natural Ventilation Model in the [[O-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$ACH_{natural}(t) = ACH_0 + k_{wind} \cdot v_{wind} \cdot window\_state(t)$$

### Description
Natural ventilation air change rate from window opening (occupant behaviour) and stack/wind-driven infiltration.

### Method
Window state Markov chain, stack effect
