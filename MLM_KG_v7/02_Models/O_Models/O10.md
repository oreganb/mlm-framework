# O10 — Smart Appliance Price Response Model

## Purpose
Smart Appliance Price Response Model in the [[O-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P_{shifted}(t) = P_{scheduled}(t) \cdot (1 - \epsilon \cdot \Delta\pi(t))$$

### Description
Load shifting by smart appliances in response to price signal Δπ = (π_current - π_ref)/π_ref. Elasticity ε from survey data.

### Method
Price elasticity, Markov decision
