# O11 — Behavioural Flexibility Willingness Model

## Purpose
Behavioural Flexibility Willingness Model in the [[O-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$flex\_score = \beta_0 + \beta_1 \cdot income + \beta_2 \cdot awareness + \beta_3 \cdot incentive$$

### Description
Household flexibility willingness score 0–1 based on income, energy awareness level, and incentive level. Gates demand response participation.

### Method
Survey-based regression, willingness-to-participate

## Weights & Parameters

- **W-O11-B0:** β_0 = 0.25 baseline
- **W-O11-B1:** β_1 = 0.15/income_bracket
- **W-O11-B2:** β_2 = 0.4
- **W-O11-B3:** β_3 = 0.02/€ incentive
