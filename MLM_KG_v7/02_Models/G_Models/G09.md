# G09 — Protection Scheme & Recloser Model

## Purpose
Protection Scheme & Recloser Model in the [[G-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$trip\_flag = \mathbf{1}[I > I_{trip}] \lor \mathbf{1}[V < V_{min}] \lor storm\_flag$$

### Description
Protection relay trip logic: overcurrent, undervoltage, or extreme weather trigger. Recloser attempts reconnection after 30s delay.

### Method
Threshold relay logic, time-overcurrent

- **W-G09-VMIN_PROT:** V_min protection = 0.85 pu (hard trip)
