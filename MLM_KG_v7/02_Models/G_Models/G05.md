# G05 — Power Factor Model

## Purpose
Power Factor Model in the [[G-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$PF = \cos(\phi) = P / \sqrt{P^2 + Q^2}$$

### Description
Power factor from active and reactive power. Inverter-based DERs can provide reactive power support (Q injection) to improve PF and support voltage.

### Method
Complex power calculation
