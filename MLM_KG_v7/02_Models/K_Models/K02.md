# K02 — EPC Compliance Model

## Purpose
EPC Compliance Model in the [[K-Series]]. Layer: **RL/Q-Learning**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$EPC\_compliant = \mathbf{1}[EPC\_rating \geq EPC_{threshold}]$$

### Description
Checks building EPC rating against regulatory thresholds. EU Energy Performance of Buildings Directive (EPBD) requires minimum EPC C by 2030, B by 2033 for Irish residential.

### Method
Regulatory threshold check
