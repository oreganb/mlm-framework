# B04 — EV Charging Demand Prediction Model

## Purpose
EV Charging Demand Prediction Model in the [[B-Series]]. Layer: **AI/ML**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P_{EV\_demand}(t) = P_{charger} \cdot \frac{SoC_{target} - SoC_{return}}{E_{bat,EV}} \cdot \frac{E_{bat,EV}}{\Delta t_{avail}}$$

### Description
EV charging power needed to reach target SoC within available parking window. Spread optimally over available time by C01.

### Method
LSTM on trip patterns, schedule-aware
