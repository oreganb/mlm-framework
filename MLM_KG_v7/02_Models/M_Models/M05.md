# M05 — Time-of-Use Tariff Model

## Purpose
Time-of-Use Tariff Model in the [[M-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$TOU_{rate}(t) = \begin{cases} \pi_{peak} & t \in peak\_hours \\ \pi_{off} & t \notin peak\_hours \end{cases}$$

### Description
Residential TOU tariff with peak (07:00–23:00) and off-peak (23:00–07:00) rates. Night saver tariff in Ireland.

### Method
Tariff schedule lookup
