# M11 — Feed-in Tariff Model

## Purpose
Feed-in Tariff Model in the [[M-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$R_{FIT}(t) = E_{export}(t) \cdot FIT_{rate}$$

### Description
Revenue from solar PV export under Irish Microgeneration Support Scheme (MSS). Fixed export rate per kWh, capped at annual limit.

### Method
Regulatory tariff schedule

- **W-M11-CAP:** Annual cap: 2,500 kWh exported at FIT rate
