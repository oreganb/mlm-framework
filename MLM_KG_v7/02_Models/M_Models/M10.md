# M10 — Flexibility Market Price Model

## Purpose
Flexibility Market Price Model in the [[M-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P_{flex}(t) = \eta_{flex} \cdot SMP(t) \cdot \Delta P_{avail}(t)$$

### Description
Flexibility market clearing price as function of SMP and available flexibility volume. η_flex captures market efficiency ratio.

### Method
Marginal clearing price, regression on SMP
