# M01 — Day-Ahead Price Forecast Model

## Purpose
Day-Ahead Price Forecast Model in the [[M-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P^{DA}_{t+1} = \alpha P^{DA}_t + \beta P^{ID}_t + \gamma X_t + \epsilon_t$$

### Description
Day-ahead electricity price forecast. α=0.72 (AR), β=0.31 (MA) calibrated on SEMO Irish market. X_t includes carbon price and weather-demand correlation.

### Method
ARIMA, LSTM, gradient boosting
