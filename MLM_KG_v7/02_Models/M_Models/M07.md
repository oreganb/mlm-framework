# M07 — P2P Energy Price Mechanism Model

## Purpose
P2P Energy Price Mechanism Model in the [[M-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P_{P2P}(t) = \frac{P_{sell,max}(t) + P_{buy,min}(t)}{2}$$

### Description
P2P clearing price as mid-market between highest seller ask and lowest buyer bid within the energy community. Bounded by [FIT_rate, retail_rate].

### Method
Double auction, mid-market pricing
