# T06 — Radiator Hydronic Circuit Model

## Purpose
Radiator Hydronic Circuit Model in the [[T-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**. Part of the building thermal envelope and HVAC modelling stack.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$Q_{rad} = k_{rad} \cdot LMTD = k_{rad} \cdot \frac{(T_{flow} - T_{in}) - (T_{return} - T_{in})}{\ln((T_{flow}-T_{in})/(T_{return}-T_{in}))}$$

### Description
Radiator output from Log-Mean Temperature Difference (LMTD) between flow/return water and room temperature. k_rad = radiator heat transfer coefficient.

### Method
hydronic RC, log-mean temperature
