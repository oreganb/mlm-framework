# T07 — PCM Thermal Storage Model

## Purpose
PCM Thermal Storage Model in the [[T-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**. Part of the building thermal envelope and HVAC modelling stack.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$C_{eff}(T) = C_{sensible} + L_{PCM} \cdot \frac{d\alpha}{dT}$$

### Description
Phase Change Material thermal storage: effective capacitance includes latent heat L_PCM during phase transition. α = liquid fraction 0→1 over melting temperature range.

### Method
RC with phase change, NTU-ε
