# T03 — Heat Pump Thermal Model

## Purpose
Heat Pump Thermal Model in the [[T-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**. Part of the building thermal envelope and HVAC modelling stack.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$Q_{HP}(t) = P_{HP}(t) \cdot COP(T_{in}, T_{out})$$

### Description
Heat pump thermal output = electrical input power × COP. Electrical power P_HP comes from C01 dispatch. COP from T02.

### Method
RC + COP coupled
