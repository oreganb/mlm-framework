# T04 — Solar Thermal Gain Model

## Purpose
Solar Thermal Gain Model in the [[T-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**. Part of the building thermal envelope and HVAC modelling stack.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$Q_{solar}(t) = G_{POA}(t) \cdot A_{window} \cdot SHGC \cdot (1 - shading)$$

### Description
Solar heat gain through glazing: irradiance × window area × Solar Heat Gain Coefficient × (1 - shading factor). Summed across all orientations.

### Method
irradiance × window area × SHGC

## Weights & Parameters

- **W-T04-SHGC:** SHGC = 0.6 (double glazing, standard Irish spec)
- **W-T04-SHGC-TRIPLE:** SHGC = 0.5 (triple glazing, post-2010 A-rated)
