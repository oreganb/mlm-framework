# T09 — Underfloor Heating Model

## Purpose
Underfloor Heating Model in the [[T-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**. Part of the building thermal envelope and HVAC modelling stack.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$C_{slab} \frac{dT_{slab}}{dt} = Q_{UFH,input} - Q_{loss,floor} - Q_{to\_room}$$

### Description
Underfloor heating as thermal mass RC model: slab capacitance charges from UFH water circuit, discharges to room. High thermal mass creates inherent storage.

### Method
RC slab with embedded pipes
