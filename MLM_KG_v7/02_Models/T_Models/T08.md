# T08 — Hot Water Cylinder (DHW) Model

## Purpose
Hot Water Cylinder (DHW) Model in the [[T-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**. Part of the building thermal envelope and HVAC modelling stack.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$C_{cyl} \frac{dT_{cyl}}{dt} = Q_{heater}(t) - Q_{draw}(t) - Q_{loss}(t)$$

### Description
Stratified hot water cylinder: balance of heater input, DHW draw demand, and standing heat losses. Temperature target T_DHW = 55°C (Legionella prevention).

### Method
stratified RC thermal
