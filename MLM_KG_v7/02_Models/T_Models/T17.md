# T17 — Thermal Inertia & Lag Model

## Purpose
Thermal Inertia & Lag Model in the [[T-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**. Part of the building thermal envelope and HVAC modelling stack.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$\tau_{thermal} = R_{env} \cdot C_{zone}, \quad T_{lag}(t) = T_{\infty}(1 - e^{-t/\tau})$$

### Description
Building thermal time constant τ = R × C. Characterises how quickly the building responds to heating/cooling. High τ = high inertia = slow response but good flexibility potential.

### Method
time-constant analysis

## Weights & Parameters

- **W-T17-TAU-HIGH:** τ > 10h: high inertia, long pre-heat window (suitable for day-ahead flex)
- **W-T17-TAU-LOW:** τ < 3h: low inertia, only short-window flexibility possible

## AI Context (Cursor Alignment)
τ_thermal determines the flexibility horizon for the building. High-mass buildings (stone, concrete) have τ ≈ 12–20h — pre-heat 8h before peak tariff window. Lightweight timber buildings: τ ≈ 2–4h.
