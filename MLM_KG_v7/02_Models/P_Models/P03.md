# P03 — Flexibility Market Bidding Model

## Purpose
Flexibility Market Bidding Model in the [[P-Series]]. Layer: **RL/Q-Learning**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$bid = (Flex^{\uparrow}(t), \; bid\_price(t)), \quad bid\_price = C_{marginal} + margin$$

### Description
Flexibility bid into DSO/TSO market: volume = Flex↑ from C01, price = marginal cost of providing flexibility + profit margin.

### Method
Strategic bidding, merit order
