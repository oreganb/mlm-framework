# P08 — Portfolio Aggregation & Market Bid Model

## Purpose
Portfolio Aggregation & Market Bid Model in the [[P-Series]]. Layer: **RL/Q-Learning**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$Flex^{\uparrow}_{portfolio} = \sum_i Flex^{\uparrow}_i \cdot flex\_willingness_i \cdot avail_i$$

### Description
Community-level aggregated flexibility portfolio for market submission. Weights individual building flexibility by willingness (O11) and DER availability (R09).

### Method
Portfolio aggregation, Aggregator role
