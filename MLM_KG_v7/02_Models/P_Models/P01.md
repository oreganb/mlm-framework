# P01 — P2P Energy Trading Revenue Model

## Purpose
P2P Energy Trading Revenue Model in the [[P-Series]]. Layer: **RL/Q-Learning**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$R_{P2P} = \sum_t E_{sell}(t) \cdot P_{P2P}(t) - E_{buy}(t) \cdot P_{P2P}(t)$$

### Description
P2P trading revenue for each building: energy sold at P2P price minus energy bought. Net across community gives individual surplus/deficit.

### Method
Double auction clearing, revenue calculation
