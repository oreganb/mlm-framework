# P05 — Bill Savings KPI Model

## Purpose
Bill Savings KPI Model in the [[P-Series]]. Layer: **RL/Q-Learning**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$savings = C_{baseline} - C_{actual} = \sum_t P^{baseline}_{grid}(t) \cdot \pi(t) - \sum_t P^{actual}_{grid}(t) \cdot \pi(t)$$

### Description
Bill savings = cost without DER/flexibility (baseline) minus actual cost with DER+flexibility+trading. Baseline = building with no PV/battery/DR.

### Method
Counterfactual comparison
