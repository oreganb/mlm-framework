# P06 — PED Performance KPI Model

## Purpose
PED Performance KPI Model in the [[P-Series]]. Layer: **RL/Q-Learning**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$PED\_balance = E_{gen,annual} - E_{consumed,annual}$$

### Description
Positive Energy District (PED) balance: total annual community generation minus consumption. PED_balance > 0 = Positive Energy District status achieved.

### Method
Annual energy balance accounting
