# P02 — Intraday Trading Model

## Purpose
Intraday Trading Model in the [[P-Series]]. Layer: **RL/Q-Learning**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$u_{ID}(t) = \arg\max_u R_{ID}(u, P_{ID}(t)) \text{ s.t. } SoC \in [SoC_{min}, SoC_{max}]$$

### Description
Opportunistic intraday market participation — buy cheap electricity (price < P_DA) to charge battery, sell when price spikes. Short time horizon (30-60 min).

### Method
Opportunistic dispatch, price-following
