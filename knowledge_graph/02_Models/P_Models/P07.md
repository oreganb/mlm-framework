# P07 — Response Derivative KPI Model

## Purpose
Response Derivative KPI Model in the [[P-Series]]. Layer: **RL/Q-Learning**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$RD\_portfolio = \sum_i w_i \cdot RD_i, \quad w_i = P_{rated,i} / \sum_j P_{rated,j}$$

### Description
Portfolio-level Response Derivative score: capacity-weighted average of individual asset RDs. Measures overall community flexibility responsiveness.

### Method
RD aggregation, portfolio scoring
