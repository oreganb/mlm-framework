# O06 — Lighting Demand Model

## Purpose
Lighting Demand Model in the [[O-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P_{light}(t) = P_{rated} \cdot occ\_state(t) \cdot (1 - \min(1, G_{POA}/G_{daylight\_thresh}))$$

### Description
Lighting power = rated power × occupancy fraction × (1 - daylight fraction). Natural daylight suppresses artificial lighting demand.

### Method
Occupancy × daylight availability
