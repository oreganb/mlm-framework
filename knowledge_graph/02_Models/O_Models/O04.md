# O04 — DHW Demand Model

## Purpose
DHW Demand Model in the [[O-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$Q_{DHW}(t) = \sum_{e \in events(t)} V_e \cdot \rho_w \cdot c_p \cdot (T_{DHW} - T_{cold})$$

### Description
DHW energy demand from stochastic draw events. Each event has volume V_e (litres) drawn from empirical distribution. DHW temperature T_DHW = 55°C target.

### Method
Statistical profile, Markov chain draw events
