# O08 — Occupant Heat Gain Model

## Purpose
Occupant Heat Gain Model in the [[O-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$Q_{occ}(t) = n_{occ}(t) \cdot q_{occ} \cdot activity\_factor(t)$$

### Description
Internal heat gain from occupants: number of occupants × heat output per person × activity factor. Sedentary = 80W/person; active = 120W/person.

### Method
Activity-weighted heat gain
