# O14 — Income-Energy Consumption Correlation Model

## Purpose
Income-Energy Consumption Correlation Model in the [[O-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$E_{consumption} = \alpha + \beta_1 \cdot income + \beta_2 \cdot floor\_area + \epsilon$$

### Description
Income-energy consumption regression. Low-income households may be energy poor (under-heating) or have older less efficient appliances (over-consumption).

### Method
Regression, energy poverty indicator

- **W-O14-BETA1:** β_1 = 0.12 per income bracket
- **W-O14-BETA2:** β_2 = 0.008 per m²
