# T13 — High-Temperature Heat Pump (HTHP) Model

## Purpose
High-Temperature Heat Pump (HTHP) Model in the [[T-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**. Part of the building thermal envelope and HVAC modelling stack.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$COP_{HTHP}(T_{source}, T_{sink}) = \eta_{Carnot} \cdot COP_{Carnot} = \frac{\eta_{Carnot} \cdot T_{sink}}{T_{sink} - T_{source}}$$

### Description
High-temperature heat pump for industrial process heat (>80°C). Carnot efficiency η_Carnot ≈ 0.45 for current HTHP technology. INNOV8HEAT application.

### Method
COP high-T + RC
