# T18 — Thermal Setpoint Optimisation Model

## Purpose
Thermal Setpoint Optimisation Model in the [[T-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**. Part of the building thermal envelope and HVAC modelling stack.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$\min_{T_{set}(t)} \int_{H} C_{energy}(t) dt \text{ s.t. } T_{min} \leq T_{set}(t) \leq T_{max}$$

### Description
Optimal setpoint trajectory over horizon H balancing energy cost (from M-Series prices) against comfort constraints. The thermal sub-problem of C01 MILP.

### Method
LP with RC constraints
