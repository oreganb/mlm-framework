# T16 — Heat Exchanger NTU-ε Model

## Purpose
Heat Exchanger NTU-ε Model in the [[T-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**. Part of the building thermal envelope and HVAC modelling stack.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$\varepsilon = \frac{Q_{actual}}{Q_{max}} = \frac{Q_{actual}}{C_{min}(T_{h,in} - T_{c,in})}$$

### Description
Heat exchanger effectiveness ε from NTU (Number of Transfer Units) method. NTU = UA/C_min where UA = overall heat transfer conductance.

### Method
NTU-effectiveness method
