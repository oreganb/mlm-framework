# T14 — Chiller Thermal Model

## Purpose
Chiller Thermal Model in the [[T-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**. Part of the building thermal envelope and HVAC modelling stack.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$COP_{chill} = \frac{Q_{cooling}}{P_{chiller}} = \eta_{Carnot,cool} \cdot \frac{T_{cold}}{T_{hot} - T_{cold}}$$

### Description
Chiller COP for cooling applications. Reverse Carnot efficiency. Applies to commercial buildings with cooling demand.

### Method
COP cooling, RC
