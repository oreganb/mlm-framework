# T05 — Infiltration & Ventilation Heat Loss Model

## Purpose
Infiltration & Ventilation Heat Loss Model in the [[T-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**. Part of the building thermal envelope and HVAC modelling stack.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$Q_{inf}(t) = \rho_{air} \cdot c_{p,air} \cdot ACH \cdot V_{zone} \cdot (T_{out} - T_{in}) / 3600$$

### Description
Ventilation/infiltration heat loss from air changes per hour (ACH), zone volume, and temperature difference. Negative when T_out < T_in (heat loss in winter).

### Method
ACH-based heat loss
