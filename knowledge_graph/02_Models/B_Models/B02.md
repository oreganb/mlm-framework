# B02 — Battery State of Health Degradation Model

## Purpose
Battery State of Health Degradation Model in the [[B-Series]]. Layer: **AI/ML**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$SoH(t) = 1 - \delta_{cycle} \cdot N_{cycles}(t) - \delta_{cal} \cdot e^{-E_a/RT(t)}$$

### Description
Battery SoH from two degradation mechanisms: cycle aging (δ_cycle per cycle) and calendar aging (Arrhenius term with activation energy E_a/R=6000K).

### Method
Cycle counting, Arrhenius thermal model, Rainflow

## Weights & Parameters

- **W-B02-CYCLE:** δ_cycle = 0.0001/cycle (10,000 cycle life)
- **W-B02-ARRH:** E_a/R = 6000 K (Li-ion Arrhenius)
- **W-B02-TEMP:** Calendar aging doubles every 10°C above 25°C
