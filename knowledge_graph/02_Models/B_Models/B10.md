# B10 — Battery Revenue Optimisation Model

## Purpose
Battery Revenue Optimisation Model in the [[B-Series]]. Layer: **AI/ML**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$\max_{P_{ch}, P_{dis}} \sum_t (P_{dis}(t) \cdot \pi(t) - P_{ch}(t) \cdot \pi(t)) \cdot \Delta t$$

### Description
Optimal battery charge/discharge schedule to maximise price arbitrage revenue. Subject to SoC, power, and cycle degradation constraints.

### Method
LP, price arbitrage
