# B06 — Battery Lifetime Monte Carlo Model

## Purpose
Battery Lifetime Monte Carlo Model in the [[B-Series]]. Layer: **AI/ML**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P(lifetime > T) = e^{-(T/\eta_{life})^{\beta_{life}}}$$

### Description
Battery lifetime distribution modelled as Weibull with shape β_life and scale η_life (mean lifetime). Monte Carlo over usage scenarios gives expected replacement year.

### Method
Monte Carlo simulation, Weibull lifetime distribution
