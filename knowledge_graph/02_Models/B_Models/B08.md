# B08 — Battery Thermal Limit Model

## Purpose
Battery Thermal Limit Model in the [[B-Series]]. Layer: **AI/ML**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P_{bat,max}(T_{bat}) = P_{bat,rated} \cdot f_{thermal}(T_{bat})$$

### Description
Battery maximum charge/discharge power derated as function of battery temperature. Derating prevents thermal runaway above 45°C and lithium plating below 5°C.

### Method
RC thermal model, manufacturer curves

## Weights & Parameters

- **W-B08-HOT:** Derate above 40°C: P_max × (1 - 0.05 per °C above 40)
- **W-B08-COLD:** Derate below 5°C: P_max × (1 - 0.10 per °C below 5)

## AI Context (Cursor Alignment)
Thermal derating is a hard safety constraint. In ODEON, battery temperatures can reach 40°C+ in summer (unventilated enclosures) and <5°C in winter. B08 output overrides B01's P_ch_max.
