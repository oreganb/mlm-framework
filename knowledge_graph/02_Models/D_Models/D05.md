# D05 — Smart Meter Data QA Model

## Purpose
Smart Meter Data QA Model in the [[D-Series]]. Layer: **AI/ML**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$|E_{meter,import} - E_{meter,export} - E_{net}| < \delta_{E,tol}$$

### Description
Smart meter data validated via energy balance (import - export = net consumption) and temporal consistency (no negative consumption, no gaps > 1 hour).

### Method
Energy balance check, temporal consistency
