# D02 — PV Data Plausibility Validator

## Purpose
PV Data Plausibility Validator in the [[D-Series]]. Layer: **AI/ML**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$plausible = P_{PV} \leq G_{POA} \cdot A \cdot \eta_{max} \cdot 1.1$$

### Description
PV generation plausibility: output cannot exceed theoretical maximum (irradiance × area × max efficiency + 10% margin). Cross-check with inverter clamp.

### Method
Physical bounds check, cross-validation with irradiance
