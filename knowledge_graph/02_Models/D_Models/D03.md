# D03 — Battery Data Consistency Validator

## Purpose
Battery Data Consistency Validator in the [[D-Series]]. Layer: **AI/ML**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$|SoC_{measured} - SoC_{computed}| < \delta_{SoC,tol}$$

### Description
Validates measured SoC against computed SoC from B01 Coulomb counting. Large discrepancy indicates meter fault, sensor drift, or BMS error.

### Method
Energy balance check, SoC consistency
