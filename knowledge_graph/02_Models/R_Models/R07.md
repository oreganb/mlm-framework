# R07 — Micro-Wind Turbine Model

## Purpose
Micro-Wind Turbine Model in the [[R-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P_{micro}(v) = P_{curve}(v) \cdot availability \cdot (1 - \delta_{turbine} \cdot age)$$

### Description
Micro-wind turbine power from site-specific power curve including availability factor (maintenance, storm shutdowns) and aging degradation.

### Method
Manufacturer power curve, Aran Islands site-specific
