# R03 — PV Degradation & Soiling Model

## Purpose
PV Degradation & Soiling Model in the [[R-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$\eta_{eff}(t) = \eta_{STC} \cdot (1 - \delta_{deg} \cdot age) \cdot (1 - \delta_{soil} \cdot days\_since\_rain)$$

### Description
Effective PV efficiency degraded by age (linear, 0.5%/year) and soiling (dust, salt spray). Soiling resets on rainfall event.

### Method
Linear degradation, soiling accumulation model

## Weights & Parameters

- **W-R03-DELTA_DEG:** δ_deg = 0.005/year (0.5%/yr — industry standard IEC 61215)
- **W-R03-SOILING:** δ_soil = 0.002/day coastal (W09 marine soiling) vs 0.0004/day inland
