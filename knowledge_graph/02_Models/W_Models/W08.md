# W08 — Solar Geometry Model

## Purpose
Solar Geometry Model in the [[W-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$\cos(\theta_z) = \sin(\phi)\sin(\delta) + \cos(\phi)\cos(\delta)\cos(\omega)$$

### Description
Solar zenith angle from latitude φ, solar declination δ, and hour angle ω. Fully deterministic astronomical calculation — no uncertainty.

### Method
PVLIB/astronomical ephemeris, deterministic
