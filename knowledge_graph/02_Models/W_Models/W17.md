# W17 — Microclimate Correction Model

## Purpose
Microclimate Correction Model in the [[W-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$\hat{x}_{corrected} = F^{-1}_{obs}(F_{NWP}(x_{NWP}))$$

### Description
Quantile mapping bias correction: maps NWP CDF to observed local CDF. Applied to all W-Series outputs for site-specific microclimate adjustment.

### Method
Bias correction, quantile mapping

## Weights & Parameters

- **W-W17-QMAP:** Quantile mapping with 100 quantile bins
- **W-W17-MARINE:** Aran Islands correction: +0.5°C temperature, -8% irradiance vs NWP (validated 2024)
