# W02 — Ambient Temperature Model

## Purpose
Ambient Temperature Model in the [[W-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$T_{out}(t) \sim \mathcal{N}(\mu_{T,t}, \sigma_{T,t}^2)$$

### Description
Outdoor temperature modelled as Gaussian with time-varying mean and variance. Mean from NWP forecast; variance from ensemble spread.

### Method
ARIMA, ensemble, regression
