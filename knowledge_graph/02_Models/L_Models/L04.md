# L04 — Appliance Load Model

## Purpose
Appliance Load Model in the [[L-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P_{appliance}(t) = \sum_i P_{rated,i} \cdot on\_off_i(t) \cdot \eta_i$$

### Description
Total appliance power from sum of individual appliance schedules. Each appliance has rated power and duty cycle efficiency factor.

### Method
Weighted sum of appliance schedules
