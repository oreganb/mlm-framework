# L07 — EV Charging Load Model

## Purpose
EV Charging Load Model in the [[L-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P_{EV}(t) = P_{charger,rated} \cdot charging\_flag(t) \cdot (SoC_{target} - SoC_t) / \Delta t_{avail}$$

### Description
EV charging power as rate-controlled charge to target SoC within available time window.

### Method
Schedule-based, rate-controlled
