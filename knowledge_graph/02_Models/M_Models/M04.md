# M04 — Distribution Network Tariff Model

## Purpose
Distribution Network Tariff Model in the [[M-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$C_{network}(t) = \tau_{DUoS}(t) \cdot P_{grid}(t) \cdot \Delta t$$

### Description
Distribution Use of System (DUoS) charges applied per kWh of grid import/export. Time-varying tariff bands (red/amber/green) from ESB Networks (Ireland).

### Method
Tariff schedule lookup, time-varying charges

## Weights & Parameters

- **W-M04-RED:** Red band: 0.18 €/kWh (17:00–19:00 weekdays)
- **W-M04-AMBER:** Amber: 0.08 €/kWh
- **W-M04-GREEN:** Green: 0.02 €/kWh (night)
