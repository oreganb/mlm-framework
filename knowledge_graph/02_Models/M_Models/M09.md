# M09 — Carbon Price Model

## Purpose
Carbon Price Model in the [[M-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$C_{carbon}(t) = P_{ETS}(t) \cdot E_{emission}(t)$$

### Description
Carbon cost from EU ETS allowance price applied to net grid emissions. ETS price forecast via ARIMA on historical EU ETS spot price.

### Method
EU ETS price forecast, ARIMA
