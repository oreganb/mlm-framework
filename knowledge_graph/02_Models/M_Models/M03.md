# M03 — System Marginal Price Model

## Purpose
System Marginal Price Model in the [[M-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$SMP(t) = f(L_{system}(t), G_{RES}(t), G_{fossil}(t))$$

### Description
System Marginal Price from merit order stack: the price of the most expensive generator dispatched to meet system load. Drives market bidding strategy.

### Method
Merit order simulation, regression
