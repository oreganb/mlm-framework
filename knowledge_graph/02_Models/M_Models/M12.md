# M12 — Market Ensemble Generator

## Purpose
Market Ensemble Generator in the [[M-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$P^{(\omega)}_{DA}(t) = \hat{P}_{DA}(t) + \epsilon^{(\omega)}, \quad \epsilon \sim \mathcal{N}(0, \Sigma_M)$$

### Description
Price uncertainty ensemble: N=50 price path realisations generated by bootstrapping from historical price forecast errors. Covariance Σ_M preserves intraday price autocorrelation.

### Method
Bootstrap, correlated sampling
