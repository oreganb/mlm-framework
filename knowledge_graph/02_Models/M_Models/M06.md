# M06 — Demand Response Incentive Model

## Purpose
Demand Response Incentive Model in the [[M-Series]]. Layer: **Stochastic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$DR_{payment} = \Delta P_{shed} \cdot t_{shed} \cdot \pi_{DR}$$

### Description
Payment for demand response event: shed power × duration × DR payment rate. Rate from DSO/TSO programme (e.g., SmartGrid Ireland SEAI programmes).

### Method
Programme lookup, payment calculation
