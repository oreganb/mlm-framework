# G01 — Feeder Power Flow Model

## Purpose
Feeder Power Flow Model in the [[G-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$V_{drop} = I \cdot Z_{line} = I \cdot (R_{line} + jX_{line})$$

### Description
Feeder voltage drop from net current and line impedance. Used to determine hosting capacity and detect voltage limit violations.

### Method
Simplified power flow, Kirchhoff's laws
