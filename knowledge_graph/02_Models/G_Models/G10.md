# G10 — EV Grid Impact Model

## Purpose
EV Grid Impact Model in the [[G-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$V_{impact}(t) = \Delta V_{EV}(t) = I_{EV}(t) \cdot R_{line}$$

### Description
Voltage impact of simultaneous EV charging on feeder. High EV penetration can cause voltage sag, especially on Aran Islands weak grid.

### Method
Load flow with EV charging loads
