# G12 — Community Energy Balance Model

## Purpose
Community Energy Balance Model in the [[G-Series]]. Layer: **Deterministic**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$EC\_balance(t) = \sum_i P_{gen,i}(t) - \sum_i P_{load,i}(t)$$

### Description
Community-level energy balance across all O-CEI buildings. Positive balance = surplus for export/storage; negative = deficit requiring import.

### Method
Aggregated power flow, energy sharing
