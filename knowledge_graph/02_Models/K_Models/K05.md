# K05 — Policy Alignment & Incentive Eligibility Model

## Purpose
Policy Alignment & Incentive Eligibility Model in the [[K-Series]]. Layer: **RL/Q-Learning**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$eligible\_schemes = \{s \in S : criteria_s(building) = True\}$$

### Description
Checks each building against eligibility criteria for all available Irish and EU energy incentive schemes (SEAI grants, MSS FIT, Warmer Homes, etc.).

### Method
Regulatory rule engine, eligibility check
