# K01 — Avoided Carbon Model

## Purpose
Avoided Carbon Model in the [[K-Series]]. Layer: **RL/Q-Learning**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$CO_{2,avoided} = (E_{ref} - E_{use}) \times EF_{marginal}$$

### Description
Avoided CO₂ from difference between reference (no-DER) energy use and actual energy use, multiplied by marginal emission factor. EF_marginal = 0.295 kgCO₂/kWh (Ireland 2024 SEAI).

### Method
Counterfactual carbon accounting
