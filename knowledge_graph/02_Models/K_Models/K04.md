# K04 — Carbon Budget Tracker

## Purpose
Carbon Budget Tracker in the [[K-Series]]. Layer: **RL/Q-Learning**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$budget\_remaining(t) = budget_{annual} - \sum_{\tau=0}^{t} CO_{2,net}(\tau)$$

### Description
Running carbon budget: annual CO₂ target minus cumulative net emissions to date. Alerts when budget is at risk of being exceeded.

### Method
Cumulative carbon accounting
