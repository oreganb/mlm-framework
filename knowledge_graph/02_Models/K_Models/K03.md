# K03 — EU ETS Reporting Model

## Purpose
EU ETS Reporting Model in the [[K-Series]]. Layer: **RL/Q-Learning**.

## Formula

### LaTeX
$$ETS\_liability = \max(0, E_{emissions} - ETS_{allocation}) \times P_{ETS}$$

### Description
EU Emissions Trading System liability from emissions above free allocation multiplied by ETS allowance price. Relevant for commercial/industrial buildings in ODEON.

### Method
Emissions accounting, ETS framework
