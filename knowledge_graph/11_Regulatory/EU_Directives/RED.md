# RED III — Renewable Energy Directive (2023)

## Summary
Sets binding 42.5% renewable energy target for EU by 2030 (with aspiration to 45%). Introduces energy community rights, prosumer rights, and renewable PPAs. Directly relevant to O-CEI/ODEON as a Renewable Energy Community and Positive Energy District.

## Key Articles Relevant to MLM

| Article | Requirement | MLM Compliance Point |
|---|---|---|
| Art. 2(16) | Renewable Energy Community (REC) definition | ODEON O-CEI qualifies as REC — [[09_Applications/ODEON]] |
| Art. 22 | REC rights: participate in generation, sharing, storage | [[02_Models/G_Models/G12]] community energy balance; [[02_Models/M_Models/M07]] P2P pricing |
| Art. 15a | Prosumer rights: self-consumption, storage, sell excess | [[02_Models/R_Models/R01]] PV; [[02_Models/B_Models/B01]] storage; [[02_Models/M_Models/M11]] FIT |
| Art. 24 | Renewable heating and cooling: 1.1% annual increase | [[02_Models/T_Models/T03]] heat pump; [[02_Models/T_Models/T13]] HTHP (INNOV8HEAT) |
| Art. 31 | Guarantees of Origin (GoO) for renewable electricity | [[02_Models/K_Models/K01]] renewable generation tracking |
| Art. 20a | Grid integration: flexibility, storage, smart charging | [[02_Models/C_Models/C01]] flexibility dispatch; [[02_Models/B_Models/B05]] V2G |

## MLM Models That Demonstrate Compliance

- **[[02_Models/G_Models/G12]]** — Community Energy Balance: quantifies shared renewable generation within the O-CEI (Art. 22)
- **[[02_Models/M_Models/M07]]** — P2P Price Mechanism: enables intra-community renewable energy sharing
- **[[02_Models/P_Models/P06]]** — PED Performance KPI: demonstrates net renewable surplus (Positive Energy District)
- **[[02_Models/R_Models/R01]], [[02_Models/R_Models/R02]]** — PV and Wind Generation: renewable generation quantification (Art. 31 GoO basis)
- **[[02_Models/T_Models/T03]]** — Heat Pump Thermal Model: renewable heating (Art. 24)
- **[[02_Models/B_Models/B05]]** — V2G: grid flexibility contribution (Art. 20a)

## O-CEI / PED Connection
The O-CEI/ODEON project on Aran Islands is structured as a Renewable Energy Community under RED Art. 22. The P06 PED balance KPI is the primary demonstration metric for community renewable surplus.
