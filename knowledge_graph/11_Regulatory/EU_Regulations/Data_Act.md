# EU Data Act — Regulation (EU) 2023/2854

## Summary
The Data Act gives users rights to access data generated by IoT devices (smart meters, sensors, EV chargers) and to share that data with third parties. Directly relevant to ODEON smart meter and sensor data pipeline, and to FLEXUS as a data-processing platform.

## Key Articles Relevant to MLM

| Article | Requirement | MLM Compliance Point |
|---|---|---|
| Art. 4 | User right to access IoT-generated data | Smart meter data (DS-SMART-METER) accessible to occupant on demand |
| Art. 5 | Right to share data with third parties | FLEXUS platform as authorised third party for ODEON data |
| Art. 23 | Interoperability requirements for data spaces | MLM KG JSON/Turtle exports as machine-readable data model |
| Art. 28 | Smart contract obligations | C-Series dispatch signals could be implemented as smart contracts |
| Art. 30 | Cloud switching — avoid lock-in | FLEXUS architecture must be cloud-agnostic |
