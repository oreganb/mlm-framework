PolyForm Noncommercial License 1.0.0, which permits
free use for academic research, education, and
non-commercial purposes.

limited to integration into commercial products or
services, use in revenue-generating deployments, or
use by for-profit organisations beyond personal
research — requires a separate commercial licence.

## Note on Horizon Europe obligations

Parts of this framework were developed under Horizon
Europe and SEAI RD&D funded projects including O-CEI
(Grant No. 101093003). Commercial licensing terms
will reflect any open access or IP obligations arising
from those funding instruments. Tyndall's technology
transfer office will advise on applicable terms at
the time of enquiry.
